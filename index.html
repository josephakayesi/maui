Aspiring Architect and urban enthusiast with a keen interest in using design to re-imagine the future of our cities and communities.

As a current Architecture graduate student at UC Berkeley, she is enjoying a program that spans from developing critical design thinking skills to building knowledge in sustainability and innovative design strategies.


With interest in four sectors that are a big part of Millicent’s design thinking — community, sustainability, affordable housing, and health — she seeks to deliver greater impact to the built environment and create livable communities for all
